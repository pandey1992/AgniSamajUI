Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines Agni Samāj’s guidelines on refunds and cancellations to ensure clarity, fairness, and transparency for all our learners.

Cancellation Policy

Student-Initiated Cancellations

Once registered, students cannot cancel their course enrollment.

Agni Samāj-Initiated Cancellations

Agni Samāj reserves the right to cancel any course due to low enrollment, instructor unavailability, or other unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, affected students will be notified promptly and offered the option to enroll in a future course.

Refund Policy

Refund Eligibility

All course registration fees are non-refundable once paid.

Contact Us

If you have questions or need further clarification regarding our refund and cancellation policies, please visit our Contact Us page to get in touch.
